Common Questions

We get it.
This is a big decision.

Will my parent feel like they're being watched?
No — and this is our core design principle. StayCara uses radar-based sensors, not cameras. There's nothing to wear, no buttons to press, and most sensors are small enough to blend into a bookshelf or nightstand. Many of our clients' parents forget the system is even there.
How is this different from a Ring camera or Nest?
Consumer smart home products are built for security, not senior wellness. They require cameras, apps your parent has to learn, and constant DIY maintenance. StayCara is a fully managed service — we handle everything, designed specifically around the health patterns that matter for aging adults.
What's included in the monthly fee?
Everything. Our team monitors your parent's system 24/7 and contacts you proactively if something looks off. You also receive weekly wellness reports, smart alerts for unusual patterns, and full hardware maintenance. If a sensor needs replacing, we handle it — you never troubleshoot anything.
Why does setup cost $3,500+?
You could buy the sensors yourself — but making them work reliably as a monitoring system is the hard part. Your setup fee covers all hardware, professional installation, room-by-room calibration, behavioral profiling for your parent's specific routines, dashboard configuration, family training, and a 30-day tuning period. It's typically 15–25 hours of expert work before monitoring even begins.
Do you use cameras?
Our core system is completely camera-free. We use invisible radar sensors that detect presence and movement without recording any images. Bathrooms and bedrooms are never monitored by cameras, period. For families who want it, we offer optional cameras for common areas — and your parent can turn them off anytime.
Do you serve areas outside Scottsdale?
We currently serve the greater Phoenix and Scottsdale metro area and are expanding soon. If you're outside our current service area, reach out anyway — we may be able to accommodate you or add you to our expansion waitlist.
